Simple Future Tense: Generality

Learn to talk about future actions, plans, and predictions

Introduction to Simple Future

üìö What is the Simple Future Tense?

Definition:

The Simple Future tense is used to talk about actions or events that will happen in the future.

When to use:
  • Predictions about the future
  • Spontaneous decisions
  • Promises and offers
  • Future plans and intentions
Examples:
I will work tomorrow.
She will visit her grandmother next week.
They will play soccer this weekend.
He will study for his exam.

Basic Structure

üî§ How to Form the Simple Future

Basic Rule:

Use "will" + base form of the verb for all subjects.

Subject Conjugation Example (work)
I will work I will work tomorrow.
You will work You will work here.
He will work He will work at a bank.
She will work She will work hard.
It will work It will work well.
We will work We will work together.
They will work They will work late.
Negative Form:

Use "will not" (won't) + base form of verb.

I will not work tomorrow.
She will not work here.
They will not work late.
Question Form:

Use "will" + subject + base form of verb.

Will you work tomorrow?
Will she work hard?
Will they work together?

üî§ Conjugation of "To Be" in Future

Subject Conjugation Example

Negative Form:

Use "will not be" (won't be).

I will not be a doctor.

They will not be here.
Question Form:

Use "will" + subject + "be".

Will I be late?
Will she be a teacher?
Will they be ready?

Common Irregular Verbs in Future

‚ö†Ô∏è Irregular Verb Conjugations in Future

About Irregular Verbs in Future:

All irregular verbs use "will" + base form in the Simple Future tense. The irregularity doesn't affect the future form.

Verb Future Form Example
go will go I will go to school tomorrow.
do will do He will do his homework.
have will have She will have a cat.
eat will eat We will eat dinner at 7 PM.
see will see They will see a movie tomorrow.
take will take I will take a bus to work.
More Examples:
I will go to the gym tomorrow.
She will do her homework after school.
We will have a great time at the party.
He will eat breakfast at 8 AM.
