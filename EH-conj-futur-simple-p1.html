I. 1. Introduction to the Simple Future

📘 What is the Simple Future Tense?

📘 Definition:

The Simple Future tense is used to talk about actions or events that will happen in the future. It expresses predictions, spontaneous decisions, promises, and future plans.

💡 When to use:
  • Predictions: It will rain tomorrow.
  • Spontaneous decisions: I'll answer the phone.
  • Promises & offers: I will help you with your homework.
  • Future plans & intentions: We will travel to Spain next year.
I will work tomorrow.
She will visit her grandmother next week.
They will play soccer this weekend.
He will study for his exam.

I. 2. Basic Structure

🔧 Form: will + base form of the verb

📘 Rule:

For all subjects (I, you, he, she, it, we, they), the Simple Future is formed with will + base verb. The form never changes.

SubjectConjugationExample (work)
Iwill workI will work tomorrow.
Youwill workYou will work here.
Hewill workHe will work at a bank.
Shewill workShe will work hard.
Itwill workIt will work well.
Wewill workWe will work together.
Theywill workThey will work late.
❌ Negative Form:

Use will not (won't) + base verb. Example: I will not work tomorrow. / She won't work here.

❓ Question Form:

Invert will + subject + base verb. Example: Will you work tomorrow? / Will they work late?

I will not (won't) go to the party.
They won't finish the project on time.
Will she call us tomorrow?

I. 3. The Verb "To Be" in Future

✨ Conjugation of "to be" (will be)

SubjectConjugationExample

Youwill beYou will be my friend.
Hewill beHe will be a teacher.
Shewill beShe will be happy.
Itwill beIt will be a beautiful day.
Wewill beWe will be here.
Theywill beThey will be students.
❌ Negative (won't be):

I will not be a doctor. / She won't be tired. / They won't be here.

❓ Question:

Will I be late? / Will she be a teacher? / Will they be ready?

I. 5. Common Irregular Verbs in Future

⚡ Irregular verbs use will + base form

All irregular verbs keep the base form after "will". The irregularity does not affect the future tense.

VerbFuture FormExample
gowill goI will go to school tomorrow.
dowill doHe will do his homework.
havewill haveShe will have a cat.
eatwill eatWe will eat dinner at 7 PM.
seewill seeThey will see a movie tomorrow.
takewill takeI will take a bus to work.
I will go to the gym tomorrow.
She will do her homework after school.
We will have a great time at the party.
