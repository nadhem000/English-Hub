III. 1. Negative Forms in Future Continuous

🚫 How to form negative sentences

📘 Basic negative structure:

Subject + will not + be + verb‑ing. Contraction: won't.

I will not be working at 5 PM tomorrow.
She won't be reading a book tonight.
They will not be cooking dinner at that time.

III. 2. Negative with 'To Be' and 'To Have'

🔹 Special cases

With 'to be' as main verb: will not be being. Example: I will not be being careful tomorrow.

With 'to have': will not be having. Example: She won't be having a party next weekend.

He won't be being patient during the meeting.
We will not be having classes at that time.

III. 3. Passive Voice in Future Continuous

📦 Form: will be being + past participle

📘 Passive structure:

Subject + will be being + past participle (+ by agent). Example: The house will be being built by workers at that time.

A letter will be being written by me at that time.
The room will be being cleaned by you tomorrow.
The car will not be being fixed by him this afternoon.
Will the report be being written by the team?
