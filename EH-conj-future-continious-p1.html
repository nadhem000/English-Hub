I. 1. Introduction to Future Continuous

⏳ What is the Future Continuous Tense?

📘 Definition:

The Future Continuous tense is used to talk about actions that will be in progress at a specific time in the future. It often expresses duration, prediction, or polite questions about future plans.

💡 When to use:
  • Action in progress at a specific future time: I will be working at 5 PM tomorrow.
  • Predicting or guessing future events: She will be studying when you arrive.
  • Polite inquiries about plans: Will you be using the car tomorrow?
  • Simultaneous future actions: They will be traveling while we are working.
I will be working at 5 PM tomorrow.
She will be studying when you arrive.
They will be traveling all next week.
He will be sleeping at midnight.

I. 2. Basic Structure

🔧 Form: will be + verb‑ing

📘 Rule:

For all subjects (I, you, he, she, it, we, they), the Future Continuous is formed with will be + present participle (-ing form). Negative: add not after will. Questions: invert will and the subject.

SubjectConjugationExample (work)
Iwill be workingI will be working tomorrow.
Youwill be workingYou will be working next week.
Hewill be workingHe will be working at the office.
Shewill be workingShe will be working on a project.
Itwill be workingIt will be working properly.
Wewill be workingWe will be working together.
Theywill be workingThey will be working all day.
❌ Negative Form:

Add not between will and be. Contracted: won't be.

I will not be working tomorrow.
She won't be working at that time.
❓ Question Form:

Invert will and the subject.

Will you be working tomorrow?
Will she be working at 5 PM?

I. 3. The Verb "To Be" in Future Continuous

⚖️ Using "to be" as an action verb

📘 Note:

When "to be" is the main verb, the Future Continuous becomes will be being. This form is rare but used to emphasise a temporary state or behaviour in the future.

SubjectConjugationExample
Iwill be beingI will be being careful tomorrow.
Youwill be beingYou will be being kind to everyone.
Hewill be beingHe will be being helpful all day.
Shewill be beingShe will be being patient with the children.
Itwill be beingIt will be being difficult to understand.
Wewill be beingWe will be being quiet during the movie.
Theywill be beingThey will be being friendly to new students.
I will not be being lazy. / She won't be being rude.
Will you be being helpful? / Will they be being noisy?

I. 4. The Verb "To Have" in Future Continuous

🍽️ Expressing experiences or events

📘 Note:

Will be having often means "experiencing" or "participating in" something (a meal, a meeting, a problem).

SubjectConjugationExample
Iwill be havingI will be having lunch at noon tomorrow.
Youwill be havingYou will be having a good time next week.
Hewill be havingHe will be having trouble with the computer.
Shewill be havingShe will be having a party next weekend.
Itwill be havingIt will be having an effect on everyone.
Wewill be havingWe will be having a meeting at 3 PM tomorrow.
Theywill be havingThey will be having fun at the beach next month.
I will not be having fun. / They won't be having a party.
Will you be having a good time? / Will she be having trouble?
