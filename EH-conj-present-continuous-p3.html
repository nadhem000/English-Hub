III. 1. Negative Forms in Present Continuous

🚫 Basic negative structure & types

📘 Negative formation:

We use "not" after the form of "to be" (am, is, are) to form negative sentences in the present continuous.

III. 2. Negative with "To Be" (as main verb)

⚖️ Using "to be" in negative sentences

SubjectPositiveNegative
II am being careful.I am not being careful.
YouYou are being helpful.You are not being helpful.
HeHe is being patient.He is not being patient.
SheShe is being kind.She is not being kind.
WeWe are being quiet.We are not being quiet.
TheyThey are being friendly.They are not being friendly.
I'm not being careful. / You aren't being helpful. / He isn't being patient.

III. 5. Passive Voice in Present Continuous

🔁 Structure & usage of passive voice

📘 Passive formation:

Subject + am/is/are + being + past participle (+ by agent). Use when the doer is unknown or unimportant.

Active VoicePassive Voice
I am writing a letter.A letter is being written by me.
He is fixing the car.The car is being fixed by him.
She is teaching the students.The students are being taught by her.
They are selling fruits.Fruits are being sold by them.
The house is not being built by workers.
Is the car being fixed by him?
