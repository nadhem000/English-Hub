I. 1. Negative Forms in Simple Present

🚫 Basic negative structure & types

📘 Formula (general):

For most verbs: [Subject] + do/does + not + base verb. For "to be": add "not" after the verb. For "to have": use do/does + not + have.

📘 Negative with "to be"

SubjectPositiveNegative
II am a student.I am not a student.
YouYou are my friend.You are not my friend.
HeHe is a teacher.He is not a teacher.
SheShe is happy.She is not happy.
WeWe are here.We are not here.
TheyThey are students.They are not students.
🗣️ Contractions: I'm not, you aren't / you're not, he isn't / he's not, she isn't, we aren't, they aren't.

📘 Negative with "to have" (main verb)

SubjectPositiveNegative
II have a car.I do not have a car.
YouYou have a book.You do not have a book.
HeHe has a cat.He does not have a cat.
SheShe has a dog.She does not have a dog.
WeWe have time.We do not have time.
TheyThey have money.They do not have money.
Contractions: I don't have, you don't have, he doesn't have, she doesn't have, we don't have, they don't have.

📘 Negative with other verbs (including irregular)

SubjectPositiveNegative
II work every day.I do not work every day.
YouYou like coffee.You do not like coffee.
HeHe plays soccer.He does not play soccer.
SheShe studies English.She does not study English.
WeWe live in Paris.We do not live in Paris.
TheyThey speak French.They do not speak French.
I don't go to the gym. / She doesn't do her homework. / They don't have classes today.

I. 2. Passive Voice in Simple Present

🔄 Structure: am/is/are + past participle

📘 Formula:

Subject + am/is/are + past participle (+ by + agent). The focus is on the action receiver.

Active VoicePassive Voice
I write a letter.A letter is written by me.
She teaches the students.The students are taught by her.
They build houses.Houses are built by them.
❌ Negative Passive:

Add "not" after am/is/are: The book is not read by students. / The car is not fixed by him.

❓ Question Form:

Invert subject and am/is/are: Is the book read by students? / Are the houses built by them?

English is spoken in many countries.
The windows are not cleaned every day.
