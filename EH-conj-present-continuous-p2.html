II. 1. Forming Questions in Present Continuous

❓ Basic question structure & types

📘 Question formation:

In the present continuous, we form questions by inverting the subject and the appropriate form of "to be" (am, is, are). Types: Yes/No questions, Wh- questions, and negative questions.

II. 2. Questions with "To Be"

🔨 Yes/No and Wh- questions with to be

SubjectStatementQuestion
II am studying English.Am I studying English?
YouYou are watching TV.Are you watching TV?
HeHe is working now.Is he working now?
SheShe is cooking dinner.Is she cooking dinner?
WeWe are playing soccer.Are we playing soccer?
TheyThey are waiting for the bus.Are they waiting for the bus?
What are you doing?
Where is she going?
Why are they running?

II. 5. Time Expressions in Present Continuous

⏰ Common time expressions & placement

Time ExpressionMeaningExample
nowat this momentI am studying now.
right nowat this exact momentShe is calling you right now.
at the momentcurrentlyWe are working at the moment.
currentlyat the present timeHe is currently living in Paris.
todayon this dayThey are visiting their grandparents today.
this weekduring the current weekI am working on a project this week.
📘 Placement:

Usually at the end of the sentence. For emphasis, at the beginning. In questions, at the end.

Right now, I am studying English.
Are you studying now?
